About

Short BIO

Hi I'm Katerina Kosta, a senior machine learning researcher at ByteDance AI Lab. I pursued my PhD from the Centre for Digital Music, Queen Mary University of London, conducting research on modelling dynamic variations in expressive music performance. Other research interests during my studies included custom data structures, pattern recognition and machine learning for music synthesis and analysis of perceived emotion in music audio.

I received degrees from National and Kapodistrian University of Athens (Mathematics) and Filippos Nakas Conservatory, Athens (Piano), and a Sound and Music Computing Masters from the Music Technology Group, UPF, Barcelona.
